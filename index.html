Our team conducted research on COVID-19 misinformation detection, utilizing three datasets totaling 1.6 million tweets and employing various classification methods, including machine learning, deep learning, and ChatGPT. We found that the support vector machine (SVM) model yielded the best overall performance in detecting misinformation related to the pandemic.

Factors Affecting 4-Year College Graduation Rate

For this project, I investigated the determinants of 4-year undergraduate institution's graduation rates. As a data analyst of the team, I cleaned the dataset and ran different prediction models to identify statistically significant variables impacting the rates using data analysis tools and statistics. We discovered that in-state tution and living expense increases correlate with higher graudation rates in public institutions, while private institutions consistently maintain higher graduation rates. We also found trends related to religious affiliations and city sizes, indicating potential areas for further research to enhance graduation rates.
